Arrive at the Paris airport and transfer to your hotel located in the picturesque Montmartre district. Enjoy the afternoon exploring the charming hillside of Montmartre, the famous Moulin Rouge and the Sacré-Coeur with its breathtaking views of the city. End your evening with a romantic dinner at one of the many gourmet restaurants in the area.

Start your day with a guided tour of the city of Paris, passing by the most famous monuments such as the Eiffel Tower, the Arc de Triomphe, the Place de la Concorde, the Jardin des Tuileries and the Louvre. In the afternoon, take a romantic stroll along the Seine or a river cruise to discover Paris from another angle. Visit the famous Musée d'Orsay, home to one of the world's largest collections of Impressionist and Post-Impressionist art. After visiting the museum, head to the beautiful Jardin des Tuileries for a romantic stroll and a lunch break in one of its charming cafés. In the afternoon, head to the Latin Quarter and visit the Pantheon, one of the most famous monuments in Paris.

End your romantic weekend by exploring the chic Saint-Germain-des-Prés district and shopping in the luxury fashion boutiques and local markets. Enjoy French culinary specialties at one of the many restaurants in the area. Afterwards, enjoy an evening show at the Moulin Rouge. Transfer to the airport for your return flight. Enjoy your last moments in the city of love before saying goodbye to Paris.
